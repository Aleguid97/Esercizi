Ingredients:

    For the Focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) of all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 of teaspoons instan yeast
  • 11/2 of teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) of lukewarm water
  • 1/4 of extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
    For the Toppin:
  • Medium Yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushin the top
  • 10 ounces of Brie, cut into a cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidered, thought it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method
